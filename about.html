About Bloom Valley Nursery

Bloom Valley Nursery is a family owned and operated business that has deep ties within our community. We specialize in offering High quality nursery stock for reasonable prices with excellent customer service.
Our knowledge and expertise is offered to anyone that enters our store; in hopes that their experience with Bloom Valley Nursery is pleasurable and worthwhile.

Bloom Valley Nursery has been serving southern California since 1985. What started as a hobby on a one acre dry farm has become California's premier Nursery.
With 15 acres of quality land Bloom Valley Nursery opened its doors for business.

We at Bloom Valley Nursery have always prided ourselves in having the areas most qualified employees to help every customer with all of their growing and gardening needs.
Bloom Valley Nursery now offers over 800 varieties of quality growing plants and will continue working hard to give our customers the very best quality and value available.
